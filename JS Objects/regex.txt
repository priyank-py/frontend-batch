
.  - Everything except new line
\w - All word characters a-z A-Z 0-9 _
\W - All non-word characters
\d - All digits 0-9
\D - All non digit
\b - word boundries
\B - non word boundries
\s - all white spaces (space, tab, newline)
\S - all non white spaces

^ - 
* - matches the character 0 to infinity
+ - matches the character 1 to infinity
( ) - to create group
{} - range of values
? - make group character optional
[] - range of characters or characters we expect
